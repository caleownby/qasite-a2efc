Here are some thoughts on why teams?

### **Why Teams Matter**

Organizing in teams allows for **deeper value generation** and more effective solutions. While it’s not mandatory to operate in teams, investing in them pays off. Here’s what I’ve witnessed:

- **Ownership Growth**: Teams I’ve worked with have embraced ownership, taking pride in their contributions.
- **Transparency**: Effective teams thrive on transparency, fostering trust and collaboration.
- **Time Efficiency**: I’ve seen a decrease in the time it takes to create value when teams align on purpose.

Remember, speed is valuable when it’s aligned with direction. Clarity around the value a team creates ensures they’re not just moving fast but creating something truly valuable.
